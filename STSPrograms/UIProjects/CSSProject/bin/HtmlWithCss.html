OOTY

Ootacamund abbreviated as Udhagai and popularly known as Ooty,renamed to un-anglicised former name Udagamandalam (Tamil: உதகமண்டலம்), is a town and a municipality in the Niligiris District of the Indian State of Tamil Nadu. It is located 86 km north of Coimbatore and 128 km south of Mysore and is the headquarters of the Nilgiris district. It is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills.
